Green Tea Cooler

Ingredients

  • 4-5 teaspoons of Pure Zen green tea
  • 4 cups of hot water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves and lemon slices for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil 4 cups of water in a kettle or saucepan.
  2. Add 4-5 teaspoons of Pure Zen green tea to a tea infuser or a tea strainer.
  3. Place the tea infuser or strainer in a heat-resistant pitcher.
  4. Pour the hot water over the tea leaves and steep for 5 minutes.
  5. Remove the tea infuser or strainer and let the tea cool to room temperature.
  6. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  7. Pour the cooled Pure Zen tea over the ice.
  8. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and lemon slices, if desired.
  9. Stir and enjoy your refreshing Pure Zen iced tea!

Serene Summer Sipper

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of Green Harmony tea
  • 1 cup of packed fresh spinach leaves
  • 1 cup of frozen mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup of canned or fresh coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon of honey or other sweetener (optional)

Instructions

  1. Steep the Green Harmony tea in 1 cup of hot water for 5 minutes. Strain the tea and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Add the cooled tea to a blender with the spinach leaves, frozen mango chunks, coconut milk, and honey (if using).
  3. Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy. If the smoothie is too thick, add a little more coconut milk or water.
  4. Pour the smoothie into a glass and serve immediately.

Enchanted Forest Matcha Latte

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of Green Harmony tea
  • 1/4 cup of hot water
  • 1 teaspoon of matcha powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • Sweetener, such as honey or agave nectar, to taste (optional)

Instructions

  1. Steep the Green Harmony tea in 1/2 cup of hot water for 5 minutes. Strain the tea and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Green Harmony matcha powder and 1/4 cup of hot water until frothy.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until hot but not boiling.
  4. Pour the Green Harmony tea and the frothed matcha mixture into the saucepan with the hot milk, and whisk until well combined.
  5. Add sweetener to taste, if desired.
  6. Pour the latte into a mug and serve hot.

Halcyon Vanilla Latte

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of Serene Rooibos tea
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • Honey or sweetener (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil 1/2 cup of water in a kettle or pot.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of Serene Rooibos tea to a tea infuser or teapot.
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Remove the tea infuser or strain the tea leaves.
  5. In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat until hot but not boiling.
  6. Add the vanilla extract to the hot milk and stir to combine.
  7. Pour the brewed Serene Rooibos tea into a mug.
  8. Pour the hot milk mixture over the tea.
  9. Add honey or sweetener to taste, if desired.
  10. Stir the latte and enjoy the creamy and soothing Halcyon Vanilla Latte.

Sencha Lemonade

Ingredients

  • 4-5 sencha green tea bags or 2-3 teaspoons of loose sencha green tea leaves
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup honey or sugar, or more to taste
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices and fresh mint leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil over high heat.
  2. Remove from heat and add the sencha green tea bags or loose sencha green tea leaves to the water. Steep for 3-5 minutes or until desired strength is achieved.
  3. Remove the tea bags or strain the tea leaves and let the tea cool to room temperature.
  4. In a large pitcher, combine the cooled sencha green tea, lemon juice, and honey or sugar. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add ice cubes to the pitcher and stir again.
  6. Serve the sencha lemonade over ice with lemon slices and fresh mint leaves for garnish.

Persian Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • 4 bags of Persian Rose tea
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate juice
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pitcher, brew the tea bags with 4 cups of boiling water for 5-7 minutes, or until the tea is strong and fragrant.
  2. Remove the tea bags and stir in the honey, lemon juice, and pomegranate juice until well combined.
  3. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  4. Serve the tea over ice cubes and garnish with fresh mint leaves (if desired).
